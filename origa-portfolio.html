Case 02 · Government official · New Delhi, India

FIR.ai — one data entry pass, six court forms out

Built for a government traffic investigation officer, FIR.ai automates the most paperwork-heavy part of accident casework: the Detailed Accident Report. An officer enters case, vehicle, driver, owner, insurance, and victim details once — the system reuses them across every required court form (I, V, VI-A, VII, VIII, X) and outputs a formatted, ready-to-file .docx packet as a direct download.

Case 03 · Higher education · Campus network

CuziCam — anonymous video chat, closed to one verified campus

CuziCam is a real-time anonymous video and text chat platform gated to students with a verified .edu email — built end to end by Origa, from the matching logic to the confessions wall. The product pairs students by shared interests, scores conversation etiquette through a "vibe score," and keeps the network closed to verified accounts only, meeting the "no bots, no outsiders" bar universities need before greenlighting a social product on campus.
